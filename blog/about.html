this website

I have created thrown words as a space for me to write. If you want to know more about the reasons behind this need, you can check out my first post.

me

I am Matteo Scopel. I like writing. I used to think that eventually that is what I would have ended up doing as an adult. I do it, for myself, but not as much as I should. Here I do it for you.

I think of this place as a place where I can escape the societal pressure of defining myself as a mostly working being. If that is what you want out of me, you can check my boring professional homepage, with links to contacts, social media, and my portfolio.

Here, I write about things I care about. That means many disparate things such as literature, philosophy, postcapitalism, cinema, technology.
